Community Involvement

Being an active and helping member within my communities is something that I strive for wherever I go. Since high school, some of my most prominent community roles have been my achievement of Eagle Scout, my involvement as a tutor and mentor for younger students through 121-Reach, and my role as Events Coordinator Apprentice in the University of Georgia's Filipino Student Association.

For my Eagle Scout project, I built an outdoor learning area for Johns Creek United Methodist Church's preschool as well as restored a nearby walkway and multiple picnic tables to their original conditions. The project demanded approximately 80 hours of planning, leading, and communicating on my part and spent 9 months in development.
